Power Piano Lessons:

By Gianna Casacchia

About Me

Hello, My name is Gianna. I am 15 years old and originally from Chicago, IL I have been playing the piano for 10 years ( and the cello for 4 years). Some of my passions are music, medicine, and teaching others. I want to help people discover their own musical talent and the beauty of music itself because through my own experiences with lessons it has broadened my creative expression, improved concentration, and teached me discipline.

What To Expect

During lessons, I will instruct students on the fundamentals of the piano, music theory, and constructive techniques to help your child grow as a player.
